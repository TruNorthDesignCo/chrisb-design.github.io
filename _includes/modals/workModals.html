An event-based campaign urging Idaho natives to become organ donors, providing aid to critical patients that need assistance. Promotional pieces include print ads, a business card-sized handout, a two-sided 'facts' flyer, and a small pamphlet detailing the story of two donors whose families would be advocating at the event.

PLEASE NOTE: This module was built using CSS2 for display on several interactive information kiosks located throughout Boise State University's campus. At this time, CSS-driven animation was a new concept, and this product was highly experimental. Due to the old infrastructure, display and design will be negatively affected and will not correctly reflect its on-screen display at time of creation.
